Introduction Telerik's Sitefinity Box Application for Android (the "Sitefinity Box App") is a utility application for Telerik's proprietary website development and administration software product, the Sitefinity Content Management System (CMS) ("Sitefinity"). The Sitefinity Box App allows you to connect to a live instance of Sitefinity 6.0 (or greater), and use it for content editing and authoring of various Sitefinity modules. The Sitefinity Box app allows you to have a native content editing experience straight on your Android powered mobile device ("Mobile Device") without having to browse the website.
